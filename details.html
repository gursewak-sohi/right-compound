Description

Desert Rose Compound, is a comfortably sized secured compound characterized by its beautiful landscaping to provide a pleasant environment. It offers 175 units of four types of units ranging between three bedroom villa, three bedroom apartment, two bedroom villa, and two bedroom apartment, thus offering a wide range of units to satisfy different accommodation needs.

The compound has a strategic location, in Al Rakah Al Janubiyah, Al Khobar, on King Fahad Road, with easy access to major roads to Dammam Airport, business centers, shopping malls, hospitals, and international schools.

Desert Rose Compound offers the usual compound's services including 24/7 security system, CCTV Cameras, on-site maintenance team, Garbage Chutes, Public area housekeeping, Bus Transportation to schools, and pest control services.
